Italian Greyhounds:

The cutest and sweetest dogs around!

Italian greyhounds are both show dogs and family dogs. They are athletic, obedient, and agile. They love to play and run around. They are known to hunt small game like mice or squirrels. Affectionately known as "Iggys", many people will atest that Italian Greyhounds are a great breed of dogs. They are great family dogs as they love to play with kids. You can even have them in an apartment.

There are a couple important things to know before you adopt an Iggy. They do not like being left alone for long periods of time or cold weather. They do not grool or require lots of grooming but they can have some health issues, like brittle bones. Like with any pet, it is important to be sure you have the time and resources to properly care for an Italian Greyhound before you get one.
